This repository presents the data used to conduct the study presented in the article "Even Further Beyond the Play Your Process: Rapid Review and
Survey Study About Business Process Models in Game Design" submitted to the Journal on Interactive Systems, 2026, 17.

The Rapid Review and Snowballing data were organized through the platform Parsif al (https://parsif.al/).

The Survey questionnaire was provided through Google Forms. All the original questions and answers were in Portuguese and 
posteriorly translated into English for the article.
